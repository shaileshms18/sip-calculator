What is SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is an investment strategy where you invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It helps in averaging out the cost of investment and building wealth over time through the power of compounding.

SIP Calculation Formula

M = P × ({[1 + i]^n – 1} / i) × (1 + i)

Where: M = Maturity Amount, P = Monthly Investment, i = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate/12/100), n = Number of Months

Benefits of SIP

✅ Rupee Cost Averaging    ✅ Power of Compounding    ✅ Disciplined Investing    ✅ Flexibility    ✅ No Need to Time the Market
